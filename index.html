November 17-23, 2014

Reno Startup Week is a weeklong celebration of startups and entrepreneurship in the Biggest Little City.

Starting up

Reno Startup Week is full of startup social and educational events. Whether you are a founder, startup employee (or want to become one!) or thinking of blazing your own trail for the first time, we'd love to see you come out and meet your fellow entrepreneurs.
